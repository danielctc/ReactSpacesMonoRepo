# SPACES METAVERSE - USER SUPPORT GUIDE

## Creating an Account

**Step 1: Access the Registration Form**
Click the "Create Account" or "Register" button. The registration form will open in a modal window.

**Step 2: Enter Your Information**
• Company Name: Enter your company name (required)
• First Name & Last Name: Enter your full name (both required)
• Work Email Address: Provide a valid work email address
• Password: Create a password (minimum 8 characters)
• Confirm Password: Re-enter your password to confirm
• LinkedIn Profile: Optional field to connect your professional profile

**Step 3: Complete Security Verification**
Check the reCAPTCHA box to verify you're not a robot. This security measure protects our platform from automated accounts.

**Step 4: Set Up Your Username**
After clicking "Next: Set Your Username", you'll see a second screen. Create a unique username (lowercase letters and numbers only, max 15 characters). Your username will appear with an "@" prefix (e.g., @username). The system will check if your chosen username is available.

Note: Please select an appropriate, professional username. Your username represents you within the Spaces Metaverse community.

**Step 5: Complete Registration**
Click "Finish" to create your account. Once registration is complete, you'll be able to sign in using your email and password.

**Step 6: Verify Your Email**
After registering, check your email inbox for a verification link from Spaces Metaverse. Click the link to verify your email address. This step is required to ensure account security.

Note: If you don't see the verification email, please check your spam or junk folder. You can request a new verification email by attempting to sign in with your credentials.

## Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password, you can easily reset it:

1. Click the "Sign In" button to open the login form
2. Click the "Forgot Password?" link below the password field
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Click the "Send Reset Link" button
5. Check your email inbox for a password reset link from Spaces Metaverse
6. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password
7. Once your password is reset, you can sign in with your new credentials

Note: If you don't receive the reset email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.

## Updating Your Profile

You can update your profile information at any time:

1. Click on your profile avatar or username to open the profile menu
2. Select "Profile" to open your profile settings
3. Click the "Edit Profile" button to make changes
4. You can update the following information:
   • Display Name: Your visible name in the platform (max 15 characters)
   • Username: Your unique identifier (lowercase letters and numbers only, max 15 characters)
   • Company Name: Your organization's name
   • First Name and Last Name: Your personal details
   • LinkedIn Profile: Link to your professional profile
5. Click "Save Changes" when you're done

Note: If you change your username, the system will check if it's available before saving.

## Customizing Your Avatar

Spaces Metaverse uses Ready Player Me to help you create a personalized 3D avatar:

1. Access the avatar creator by clicking on your profile menu and selecting the avatar option
2. The Ready Player Me interface will open in a new window
3. Create your avatar by:
   • Taking a selfie to generate a look-alike avatar (requires camera permission)
   • Choosing from pre-made options
   • Customizing features including face shape, hairstyle, skin tone, and clothing
4. After customizing your avatar, click "Done" or "Save" to export it
5. Your new avatar will be automatically saved to your profile and appear in the Spaces Metaverse environment

Note: Creating your avatar requires allowing camera access if you choose the selfie option. You can always skip this step and customize manually.
